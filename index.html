Sudha murthy

Sudha Murthy, born on August 19, 1950, is an accomplished Indian author,
social worker, and philanthropist. She holds a Bachelor's degree in
Electrical Engineering and briefly worked as a computer scientist at TELCO.
In 1981, Sudha Murthy co-founded Infosys Foundation with her husband, N.R. Narayana Murthy,
focusing on social initiatives. Her writing spans children's books, novels, and short stories, often addressing societal issues.
Sudha Murthy's literary contributions and philanthropic work reflect her commitment to positive change.
